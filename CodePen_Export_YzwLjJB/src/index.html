Introduction

We are an independent non-profitable organisation trying to promote awarness about natural black hair. We recommend hair products for women on a comission basis.The money we make from commission all goes to charities who help promote natural hair awareness.We started operating in early 2015 as part of the natural hair movement.We try to help women with who decide to go natural. By no means do we force women to go natural.But we offer tips and care routines tailered to their hair texture have they decided to go natural and need help in understanding their natural curls and locks. We have experts who specialised in hair.They are able to ofefr advice and give guidence if needed.

We Offer:

Face to Face consultation

In this appointment session, our experts will tell you everything there is to know about your hair such as hair elasticity,hair porosity, hair smoothness, hair volume,hair softness, hair shine...etc. They will also determine type of your hair: 4a, 4b and 4c. 4a hair is extremely coiled and shows an S-shaped pattern. This hair type can range from being wiry and frizzy to smooth and fine-textured. 4b hair type displays a Z-shaped patternThe consultation is when we can see the hair texture when it's dry, how you wear it, and when our experts can explain all of the little .

Try-out sessions

This session is also one to one session where our experts reccommend products which are suitable for your natural hair depending on whether you hair is fully natural or recently transitioned hair.

Treatment Sessions

The treatment session is host evey thursday.The treatments we offer are karetin treatment, protein treatment, deep conditioning treatment, Morroccon hair treatments,Henna hair treatment and other hair treatment.These treatments do not involve extreme hair manupilations

Hair-Care Classes

Every Wednesday and Friday, we host a hair care class where our experts discuss hair and teach differnt types of hair styling with no hair manupilations such as braids, buns, slik styles and others.You could also discuss if you have any prefered hair style.The team will give you their professional experties.

Meet the Charities Sessions

We hold these sessions every saturday of of the first week of a month.We hold these sessions to get more donations from the public such as yourself. They are open events where anyone can come in without booking an appointment.These are important events as you will be able to meet the charities representatives and ask any questions you may have about your contributions.We highly recomend attending these events.
